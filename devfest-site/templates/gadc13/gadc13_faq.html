FAQs!

  • Q: What is a GDG DevFest?

    Inspired by the success of DevFestX in Japan, New Delhi, Bangalore and Korea, we are inviting GDG chapter across the world to organize large community developer event under the brand "GDG DevFest".

  • Q: Who can organize a GDG DevFest?

    Invitation to host GDG DevFest is being extended to active GDG chapters listed here. Ini case someone wants to organize a GDG DevFest in a city with no GDG chapter then the first step should be to start a GDG chapter in that city. In addition to the GDG chapters, we are inviting chapters who have decided to stay with the GTUG branding to also plan and organize GTUG DevFests.

  • Q: What qualifies as a GDG DevFest?

    GDG DevFest can be organized all through the year and are community run events. Therefore these events take the form desired by the community with minimal guidelines. In order to ensure these events are bigger than regular GDG meetups following are some of the recommendations: Duration of the event should be at least 6 hours more than 1 track if organizing a speaker session based event Hackathon (Single or Multi Product) - Ideally somewhere between 24-48 hours.

  • Q: What is Global GDG DevFest season?

    At the GDG Summit in Mountain View on 26th June 2012, we discussed the idea of global DevFest week with the community organizers. Proposal is to organize several simultaneous GDG DevFest events across the world in the month of September 2012. As per the discussion during the GDG Summit, we have identified 21 September-11 November as the suitable period for global DevFests.

  • Q: How can I have GDG DevFest in my region listed on the website?

    Please fill this form to let us know of your DevFest plans. All the proposals submitted through this form would be listed on the website.

  • Q: What branding to use for the GDG DevFest in my region?

    Please refer to the logo generator tools under "Branding" tab.
