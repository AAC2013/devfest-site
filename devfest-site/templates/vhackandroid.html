Android's 5th Birthday Celebration: European Best-of-Best Hackathon Series

Android became public on November 5th, 2007. That means it'll be 5 years of Android this November (2012)! Can't let this go by unnoticed! So the developer user groups (GDGs/GTUGs/AUGs) decided to organize an Android app hackathon tournament (think GADC but with higher stakes).

In September/October 2012 you can participate in a local hackathon organized by your GTUG. Each winning team receives a chance to defend their user group in a final European hackathon in London. We pay your flight and hotel. This TopGun hackathon takes place on the weekend of October 26th-28th, right after Droidcon London.

Android developers from all over Europe will come together from tens of locations and hack for 48 hours - nonstop! Prizes are to be announced.

You can follow the livestream of the major events in September and on October 26th-28th or interact with us during public Hangouts on Google+. Our developer wiki has a list of hacks.

Hashtag: #AndroidV
Remember, remember, the fifth of November!
